WELCOME TO CORNITO UNIVERSITY!

.

Renowned for its commitment to excellence, Cornito University develops its students’ professional competence, character, and social transformation. Cornito University is a learning environment that nurtures students to develop their interests and passions, and trains them to become professionally competent in established industries and emerging fields or specialization.

WHY CHOOSE CORNITO UNIVERSITY?

ad

As an innovative Philippine business institution, Cornito University believes that access to quality education is the solution to many of our country’s problems. While the enrollment rate in the Philippines has always been high, Cornito University discovered that most students do not finish tertiary education. Students were willing to learn but did not have the resources to stay in school. That is why in 2004, Cornito University was established.


Cornito University is making education accessible in key growth areas all over the country. It has transformed existing educational institutions to provide better academic, operational, and community support for all of its students. Through its efforts more students are able to earn college degrees and become globally competitive professionals.
